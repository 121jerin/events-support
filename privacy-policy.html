Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2025

Eventify respects your privacy. Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter or manage within the app is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal or usage data. The app uses SwiftData to store information (such as tasks or events) locally on your device only. This data is not sent to us or any third party.

2. Internet Connectivity

The app may connect to the internet for features such as updates or in-app purchases, but it does not transmit any user content or personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers in the app.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages and does not collect data from anyone, including children.
